Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

Overview

UniPilot is a study planning app for university students. It helps you organize courses, timetable events, deadlines, exams, study sessions, grades, and reminders.

UniPilot is designed to keep your study data on your device. The developer does not sell your data, does not use advertising tracking, and does not collect analytics about how you use the app.

Information Stored on Your Device

UniPilot may store information you choose to enter or import, including course names, course codes, ECTS, academic year, language, exam composition, grades, deadlines, exams, timetable events, study timer sessions, notification preferences, and profile settings.

This information is stored locally on your device using Apple system storage. It is used to show your study plan, calculate study summaries, schedule reminders, and keep the app working between launches.

Timetable and Course Links

If you import a timetable link, UniPilot uses that link to fetch timetable information from the timetable provider. For University of Amsterdam students, this may include requesting data from Rooster.

If you import a course link, UniPilot uses the link you provide to fetch course information from the course catalogue provider, such as the University of Amsterdam Studiegids.

These requests are made only to load the timetable or course information you asked UniPilot to import. UniPilot does not operate its own server for this data.

Notifications and Live Activities

UniPilot can ask for permission to send local notifications for upcoming deadlines, exams, and tutorial classes. You can turn these reminders on or off in the app and manage notification permission in iOS Settings.

When you start a study timer, UniPilot may show the active session through Apple Live Activities, the Dynamic Island, and the Lock Screen, depending on your device and system settings.

Maps

When you open a map from an event detail screen, UniPilot passes the event location or address to Apple Maps or Google Maps. Your use of those map services is governed by the privacy policies of those providers.

Data Collection and Tracking

UniPilot does not collect personal data for the developer, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

If future versions add account syncing, cloud backup, analytics, or other online features, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before those features are released.

Your Choices

You can edit or delete courses, deadlines, grades, timetable imports, and study sessions inside UniPilot. You can also remove the app from your device to delete locally stored UniPilot data, subject to normal iOS backup behavior.

You can change notification permission at any time in iOS Settings.

Children

UniPilot is intended for university students and is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact UniPilot through the support contact listed on the app's App Store product page.
